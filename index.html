Game Rules

  • The goal of the game is to be the first player to reach 100 points.
  • On each player's turn, they can either roll the dice or hold.
  • If a player rolls a number between 2 and 6, that number is added to their current score.
  • If a 1 is rolled, the current score resets to 0 and their turn ends.
  • Holding adds the current score to the total score and ends the player's turn.
  • The first player to reach 100 or more points wins the game!
  • Strategize carefully! Keep rolling for more points but beware of losing it all by rolling a 1.
